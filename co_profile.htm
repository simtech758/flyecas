ECAS is a low-cost airline providing scheduled, charter and cargo services within the Eastern Caribbean.

Our airline company was incorporated in St.Lucia in March 2010 and we began serving customers in June 2010 with our fleet of three state-of-the-art Beechcraft 1900D aircraft. We are based in St.Lucia and serve five other destinations: Dominica, Barbados, Grenada, St.Vincent and Trinidad.

ECAS aims to enrich the lives of the Eastern Caribbean people by providing safe, convenient and affordable air travel. We focus on efficiency in order to provide hassle-free service at low fares, creating savings for our customers by making inexpensive air travel a reality.

We at ECAS take pride in our core values of safety, convenience and reliability, as well as outstanding customer relations at an unbeatable price.

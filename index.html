ECAS is a low-cost airline providing scheduled, charter and cargo services within the Eastern Caribbean.br>
Our airline company was incorporated in St.Lucia in March 2010 and we began serving customers in June 2010 with our fleet of three state-of-the-art Beechcraft 1900D aircraft. We are based in St.Lucia and serve five other destinations: Dominica, Barbados, Grenada, St.Vincent and Trinidad.
